ABOUT

A little bit about me

I build tools and conduct research on misinformation, how it spreads, and what it takes to counter it. My work in the field has been reviewed and certified by senior experts at organizations that lead the fight against it, and as a TKS Innovate graduate I've learned to turn early ideas into things that actually ship. I put that work to the test in public: I compete in hackathons and competitions, and I win them.

Building is only half of it. I write to think problems through, I perform, and I shoot photography, disciplines that look separate but sharpen the same instincts: noticing what others miss, and caring enough to get the details right.
